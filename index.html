B

Structural Repair & Rehabilitation

Cost-effective alternatives to full replacement — extending the service life of jetties, overwater villas, and marine infrastructure through internationally-compliant repair systems.

Jetty Repair & Rehabilitation Structural beam and column repair, load-bearing restoration, and waterproofing to ACI 224R and BS EN 1504 standards.
Marine Concrete Restoration Crack classification and targeted repair using saltwater-resistant systems engineered for permanent underwater and tidal zone exposure.
Asset Life Extension Corrosion protection, surface coating, and strategic maintenance programmes that defer replacement and reduce long-term asset costs.
C

Installation & Logistics

Heavy lifting, marine transport, and precision deployment across the archipelago. Our logistics capability is purpose-built for the Maldives — managing the full supply chain from precast yard to remote atoll installation.

Marine Transport & Island Delivery Coordinated barge and vessel logistics for moving heavy precast elements to any atoll across the archipelago — including remote locations with no port infrastructure.
Heavy Lifting & Placement Specialist marine craneage and rigging capability for precise element positioning in open-water conditions — including tidal and wave-exposed environments.
Supply Chain & Precast Deployment End-to-end supply chain management — from factory scheduling and quality sign-off through to site delivery and in-water installation of precast products.
